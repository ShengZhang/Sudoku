AppWizard has created this Sudoku application for you.

This file contains a summary of what you will find in each of the files that
make up your Sudoku application.

This is an application for Sudoku game. You can input the orginal values of
Sudoku, then it will compute and generate the result for you.

Support input/output a text file. For instance:

Input file:
9,0,8,0,3,1,0,0,0,
0,0,0,0,2,0,5,0,0,
5,0,0,0,9,0,0,4,0,
0,0,0,8,0,0,0,0,5,
8,3,2,0,0,0,1,7,4,
1,0,0,0,0,7,0,0,0,
0,8,0,0,5,0,0,0,3,
0,0,7,0,8,0,0,0,0,
0,0,0,4,7,0,6,0,2

Output file:
*****Begin*****
9 7 5 6 8 1 4 2 3 
4 1 2 7 3 9 8 6 5 
8 3 6 4 2 5 9 7 1 
5 6 7 8 9 3 2 1 4 
3 2 9 1 6 4 5 8 7 
1 4 8 2 5 7 6 3 9 
2 5 3 9 1 8 7 4 6 
6 9 4 3 7 2 1 5 8 
7 8 1 5 4 6 3 9 2 
*****End*****
